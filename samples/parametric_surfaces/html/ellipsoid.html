Ellipsoid

Parametrization of an ellipsoid surface.

$f(u, v) = (x, y, z)^T$ with

  • $x = a\sin u\cos v$
  • $y = b\sin u\sin v$
  • $z = c\cos u$
where $a$, $b$ and $c$ are scaling factors in the three dimensions, $0 \le u \le \pi$, and $0 \le v \le 2\pi$.

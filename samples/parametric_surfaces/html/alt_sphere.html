Alternative sphere

Parametrization of a sphere. From a question about the 'Parametric equation of a cone' on math.stackexchange

$f(u, v) = (x, y, z)^T$ with

  • $x = a \sin(u \arctan(\frac{v}{u})) \sin(v \arctan(\frac{v}{u}))$
  • $y = b \sin(u \arctan(\frac{v}{u})) \cos(v \arctan(\frac{v}{u}))$
  • $z = c \cos(u \arctan(\frac{v}{u}))$
where $a$, $b$ and $c$ are scaling factors, $-5 \le u \le 5$ and $-5 \le v \le 5$.

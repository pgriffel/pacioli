Flower

Parametrization of a flower. From the question 'What equation will create a 3D rose curve?' on math.stackexchange

$f(u, v) = (x, y, z)^T$ with

  • $x = a \cos(bv) \cos^k(c u) \cos u \cos v$
  • $y = a \cos(bv) \cos^k(c u) \cos u \sin v$
  • $z = a \cos(bv) \cos^k(c u) \sin u$
where $a$, $b$ and $c$ are scaling factors, $0 \le u \le 2\pi$, and $0 \le v \le 2\pi$.

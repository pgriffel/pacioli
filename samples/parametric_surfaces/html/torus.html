Torus

Parametrization of a torus surface.

$f(u, v) = (x, y, z)^T$ with

  • $x = (a + b \cos u) \sin v$
  • $y = (a + b \cos u) \cos v$
  • $z = b\sin u$
where $a$ is the major radius, $b$ is the minor radius, $0 \le u \le 2\pi$, and $0 \le v \le 2\pi$.

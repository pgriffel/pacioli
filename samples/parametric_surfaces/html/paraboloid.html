Paraboloid

Plot of a paraboloid surface by revolutions.

$f(u, v) = (x, y, z)^T$ with

  • $x = u \cos v$
  • $y = u \sin v$
  • $z = a u^2$
where $a$ is a scaling factor, $0 \le u \le 5$, and $0 \le v \le 2\pi$.

The square $u^2$ for $z$ makes its unit metre squared.
